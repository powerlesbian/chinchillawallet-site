Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 24, 2025

1. Introduction

Creepy Labs (H.K.) Limited ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Chinchilla Wallet mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using Chinchilla Wallet, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We may collect the following information that you voluntarily provide:

  • Email Address: For account recovery and important notifications
  • Phone Number: For account verification and security purposes (optional)
  • Wallet Addresses: Public blockchain addresses generated within the App

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

We do NOT collect or track:

  • Device information
  • Usage analytics
  • IP addresses
  • Location data
  • Cookies or tracking technologies

3. How We Store Your Information

On-Device Storage Only: All wallet data, including private keys, transaction history, and personal information, is stored locally on your device. We do NOT store your data on our servers.

Your Responsibility: You are solely responsible for backing up your wallet recovery phrase and securing your device. If you lose your device or uninstall the App without backing up, we cannot recover your data.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the App functionality
  • Enable account recovery if you forget your password
  • Send important security notifications (if opted in)
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information
  • Share your data with third parties for marketing
  • Track your activity or behavior

5. Blockchain Transactions

Chinchilla Wallet operates on the one-chain blockchain. Important notes:

  • Public Ledger: All transactions are recorded on a public blockchain and are permanently visible
  • Wallet Addresses: Your wallet addresses are public, but not directly linked to your personal identity unless you disclose them
  • Irreversible: Blockchain transactions cannot be reversed or deleted

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: Private keys are encrypted and stored locally on your device
  • No Server Storage: Your sensitive data never leaves your device
  • Secure Communication: All network communications use encryption

However: No method of electronic storage is 100% secure. You are responsible for keeping your device and recovery phrase secure.

7. Third-Party Services

Chinchilla Wallet does NOT integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

We may link to external websites (e.g., blockchain explorers). These sites have their own privacy policies, which we do not control.

8. Children's Privacy

Chinchilla Wallet is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we discover we have collected data from a child, we will delete it immediately.

9. Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA Compliance)

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data (stored on your device)
  • Deletion: Delete your account and data by uninstalling the App
  • Portability: Export your wallet data using the App's backup feature
  • Opt-Out: Decline optional features like email notifications

To exercise these rights, contact us at hkoffice@chinchillawallet.com

10. International Data Transfers

Because all data is stored on your device, there are no international data transfers to our servers. However, blockchain data is distributed globally across the one-chain network.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
